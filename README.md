# Tinyhat

**Observability and optimization for AI skills.**

Tinyhat gives you a clearer picture of which skills your agents are actually using — and which ones might be missing.
The goal is to make skill lifecycle easier: knowing what to add, what to retire, based on what the work actually asks for.

## Why "Tinyhat"?

I keep coming back to the way humans swap roles: "put on your marketing hat."
I wonder if AI agents will land there too — a role, a hat, the skills that come with it.
Tinyhat is the observability for those skills, so a team can see what's used, what it costs, and what's worth keeping.

## Status

Pre-alpha. Design phase. No installable artifact yet.
